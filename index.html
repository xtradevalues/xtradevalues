About xTradeValues

A proprietary MLB trade value system analyzing player skill, age, contract surplus, and prospect potential across a 7-year projection window.

Values are calculated using a multi-factor model incorporating WAR projections, salary arbitration tiers, age curves, and prospect ETA discounting. Data sourced from FanGraphs.
