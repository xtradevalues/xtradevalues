About ValueSavant

A proprietary MLB trade value system analyzing player skill, age, contract surplus, and prospect potential across a 6-year projection window through 2031.

Values are calculated using a multi-factor model incorporating future performance projections, salary arbitration tiers, age curves, and prospect ETA discounting. Data sourced from public sources.

How do we score?
