About ValueSavant

A proprietary MLB trade value system analyzing player skill, age, contract surplus, and prospect potential across a 6-year projection window through 2031.

Values are calculated using a multi-factor model incorporating future performance projections, salary arbitration tiers, age curves, and prospect ETA discounting. Data sourced from public sources.

Methodology
Player Values
ValueSavant projects future performance using a weighted 4-year window of MLB/MiLB statistics, scouting grades, and expected metrics — then factors in surplus value and age across a player's remaining control window through 2031.
2026
A player's projected value for the current season
Avg
The average annual value across a player's remaining control window
Ctrl Thru
The final year a player remains under team control
Team Record Projections
Rest of season record projections are based on 2026 performance of team, as well as current and expected statistics of players on the Active Roster. Injuries/Returns/Acquisitions of impact players will influence the projections.
